# Privacy Policy — Timerz: Interval Timer

**Last updated: April 2, 2026**

## Overview

Timerz is a workout interval timer app. We take your privacy seriously — the app requires no account, no login, and your timer data never leaves your device.

## Data We Collect

### Anonymous Analytics (Firebase Analytics)
We collect anonymous usage events (e.g. "timer started", "preset used") to understand how the app is used and improve it. This data is:
- Fully anonymous — not linked to you personally
- Never sold or shared with third parties
- Automatically purged by Google after 14 months

### Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics)
If the app crashes, an anonymous crash report is sent to help us fix the bug. This includes:
- Device model and OS version
- App version
- Stack trace of the crash

No personal data is included in crash reports.

### Purchase Verification (RevenueCat)
If you purchase Timerz Pro, RevenueCat processes the transaction through Google Play or the App Store. RevenueCat receives:
- An anonymous installation identifier
- Your purchase receipt (for verification)

RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or payment details — those are handled entirely by Google Play / App Store.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
- Location data
- Health or fitness data
- Contacts, photos, or files
- Your timer configurations or workout history (stored locally on your device only)

## Data Storage

All timer configurations and workout history are stored **locally on your device**. We have no access to this data. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes it.

Timerz is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
